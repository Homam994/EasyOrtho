Notation Reference Guide
FDI / ISO 3950
Two-digit system. First digit = quadrant (1–4), second = tooth position (1–8).
11–18 = Upper Right
21–28 = Upper Left
31–38 = Lower Left
41–48 = Lower Right
Most common internationally & in Gulf.
Palmer Notation
Letter + number in a quadrant grid.
UR1–UR8 = Upper Right
UL1–UL8 = Upper Left
LL1–LL8 = Lower Left
LR1–LR8 = Lower Right
Common in UK & Commonwealth countries.
Universal (ADA)
Sequential 1–32, starting upper right.
1–8 = Upper Right (8→1)
9–16 = Upper Left (1→8)
17–24 = Lower Left (8→1)
25–32 = Lower Right (1→8)
Standard in USA and some other countries.
